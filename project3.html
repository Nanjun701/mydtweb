Project 3: Experimental Clock

Description

I love to cook and usually I need to thaw frozen meats ahead of time. It usually takes about three hours to thaw a pork belly. I wanted to represent the passage of time with the process of melting the ice.

Reflection

I find it interesting to think about replacing invisible information with a visual method. But I'm not quite sure how to get clock to reset or opacity to start at 190 every time I upload.
